SIH Project - E-Care

This project was submitted by our team in Smart India HackaThon an initiative by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. With team of 6 members developed the web app for Government of Tamil Nadu to track medical records of patient in government hospital.It is web application that tracks all the medical records and attaches them to a patient unique ID.We used JSP/Servlets with MVC Architecture to build this project.
